The Experience

A seven-night, five-round trip on the Costa del Sol, one of Europe's most established golf destinations. The format is fixed. Every tee time, transfer, and logistical detail is pre-arranged and delivered by a dedicated host. Nothing is left to chance and nothing is delegated to the guest.

Accommodation is in a 5-star hotel with individual rooms throughout. Breakfast is included daily. Private transfers handle all movement. Two hosted group dinners, on the first and final evenings, anchor the social rhythm of the week. Restaurant reservations are arranged for every other evening of the trip, though attendance and cost are at each guest's own discretion.

A dedicated rest day sits mid-itinerary by design.

The Group

Groups are between 8 and 12 guests and assembled through a deliberate curation process. Every guest completes a profile before joining, covering their professional background, lifestyle, interests, and golf. That information is used to compose groups with genuine common ground.

Friends and colleagues are welcome to book together. For those joining alone or with other people, the curation ensures they arrive among peers, not strangers. The result is a group dynamic that feels natural from the first evening.
